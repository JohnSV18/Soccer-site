Promoting Growth & Development Through Competitive Football

  • Prepare your children for their brightest future. Eastern NYC Football Club is innovating the field of youth soccer player development through our emphasis on discipline, hard work and determination that is instilled in each player at every training session, which then translates to their daily lives. We offer a unique training experience, designed to enhance individual skill, collective identity, and personal growth, which we believe is the key to participatory engagement.   • The transformative learning experiences at Eastern NYC Football Club are designed to help our players grow both on and off of the football pitch. Founded in the summer of 2016, Eastern NYC Football Club is located in Queens (New York) and reflects the vibrant energy of the Football culture that is pervasive in the lives of so many people of the area.
